02. Project Structure

CNYT

Private-equity-oriented security token used to structure contributions for CND development, reserve formation, technology, operations, and ecosystem expansion.

CND

USD-referenced, mixed-collateral casino exchange and settlement token intended for conversion into local currency, casino chips, and approved ecosystem services.

06. LONGRISE: Exclusive Participation Gateway

Role

LONGRISE AI is an online casino and auto-betting platform launched by HCF with investor approval. It is the sole current participation gateway for non-existing investors.

No public sale, unrestricted airdrop, direct foundation subscription, or external retail-access channel is currently offered.

Member Allocation

CNYT allocations are determined by membership tier, platform tenure, auto-betting usage, transaction volume, referral activity, and ecosystem contribution.

Distributions occur in phases and are governed by separate LONGRISE allocation policies.

07. Member Allocation, Buyback & 12-Month Lock

1. Allocation

CNYT is credited based on eligible LONGRISE activity.

2. Seven-Day Window

Members may resell internally or submit an irrevocable buyback request for USDT.

3. Auto-Lock

Unsettled balances automatically enter the custodial pool and lock for 12 months.

  • LONGRISE announces one fixed buyback price before each seven-day cycle and accepts all eligible requests without quantity limits.
  • Buybacks are funded from a designated portion of LONGRISE revenue and are intended to accumulate CNYT before CND launch.
  • The 12-month pool pays no periodic reward and does not grant members direct CND conversion rights.
CNYT / HCF BUSINESS WHITEPAPER9

08. Maturity, USDT Settlement & Recommitment

At 12-Month Maturity

LONGRISE announces the applicable USDT conversion price before maturity. Members receive a 14-day election period to choose one of two outcomes:

• Full USDT conversion and withdrawal
• Recommitment for a new 12-month cycle

Recovered CNYT

CNYT acquired through maturity settlement may be divided between LONGRISE’s strategic reserve pool and the member ecosystem distribution pool.

LONGRISE publishes annual aggregate totals, while cycle-specific treasury allocations remain confidential.

09. Internal P2P Marketplace

  • Current secondary trading is limited to eligible LONGRISE members through the internal P2P marketplace.
  • LONGRISE publishes a reference price and a variable permitted trading range for each trading period.
  • Seller-side fees apply; buyer-side transaction fees do not.
  • Every P2P receipt creates a fresh seven-day grace period. The buyer may resell, request buyback, or allow automatic 12-month lock-up.
  • Wash trading, linked-account activity, circular transfers, abnormal orders, and artificial volume are monitored and may be restricted.
  • Daily and monthly limits vary by membership tier, platform history, usage, and risk indicators.

12. CNYT-to-CND Conversion

One-Way Conversion

Eligible CNYT may be converted into CND. CND cannot be converted back into CNYT.

Converted CNYT is permanently burned, reducing total and circulating supply.

Pricing & Access

Conversion value uses the 24-hour CNYT TWAP and a separately published fixed conversion fee.

Access begins with approved strategic participants and may expand in phases. LONGRISE receives priority access but no preferential price.

LONGRISE member reward balances do not carry direct CND conversion rights. LONGRISE may convert only CNYT it has validly reacquired into its own strategic pool.

13. CND Reserve Framework

Casino-Asset-Centered

Operating rights, revenue rights, junket interests, exchange reserves, resort equity, and related private-market gaming assets.

Liquidity Support

Cash-equivalent and liquid digital assets support settlement and reserve operations.

110% Minimum

Aggregate risk-adjusted reserve value must remain at least 110% of outstanding CND reference value.

CNYT itself is not reserve collateral. Only capital and assets actually contributed, acquired, or contractually allocated through the CNYT structure may enter the CND reserve.

14. Reserve Valuation & Remediation

Valuation

Gaming-linked assets use a hybrid method combining independent external appraisal, actual operating performance, and discounted expected cash flows.

Formal independent valuation occurs annually, with internal quarterly monitoring and extraordinary revaluation where necessary.

Below 110%

New CND issuance and CNYT-to-CND conversion are suspended. The Foundation must restore coverage through liquidity contributions, revenue retention, exposure reduction, asset sales, or reserve restructuring.

15. Privacy Architecture

CND is designed as a privacy-preserving casino settlement token. Sender, recipient, amount, wallet relationships, and settlement activity should not be publicly exposed by default.

Private Ledger for Authorized Parties

With consent from both transaction parties, authorized key holders may access the complete transaction record as a private ledger.

Validity Proof for Third Parties

Third parties receive only a cryptographic proof that the transaction is valid, without seeing identities, amount, or confidential details.

The business whitepaper defines principles only. The blockchain and cryptographic implementation will be specified in a separate technical whitepaper.

18. Governance

Quarterly Distribution Proposal

The Foundation proposes a rate within 20%–40% of quarterly net CND operating fees.

Only holders with active eligible CNYT staking positions may vote. Voting power is based on active staked CNYT without staking-term multipliers.

Approval Rule

Quorum: 20% of eligible active staked CNYT.
Approval: simple majority of votes cast.

If no new rate is approved, the prior approved rate carries forward. The first distribution cycle defaults to 20%.

Casino Network Yield Token business framework summary based on the June 2026 integrated whitepaper.

Executive Summary

CNYT is a private-market security token created to structure capital and economic rights contributed toward the future issuance and operation of CND, a privacy-preserving casino exchange and settlement token.

CNYT does not tokenize the entire casino investment network. Only expressly allocated capital, contractual rights, operating interests, and reserve assets are included in the CNYT-CND ecosystem.

Asset Network & Value Scope

  • ~$700M: broader casino network assets, not current CND reserve collateral.
  • $10M: 1B CNYT multiplied by the $0.01 initial reference value.
  • $100M+: target ecosystem value at scale from CND exchange, payment, settlement, reserve growth, and network expansion. This is not a guarantee.

Tokenomics

  • 50% / 500,000,000 CNYT: LONGRISE member ecosystem pool.
  • 40% / 400,000,000 CNYT: existing investors and initial capital participants.
  • 10% / 100,000,000 CNYT: foundation operations and reserve.

LONGRISE Participation Gateway

LONGRISE AI is the current exclusive participation gateway for non-existing investors. No public sale, unrestricted airdrop, direct foundation subscription, or external retail-access channel is currently offered.

  • CNYT allocations are based on membership tier, platform tenure, auto-betting usage, transaction volume, referral activity, and ecosystem contribution.
  • Eligible members may trade internally through LONGRISE P2P or request buyback during a seven-day window.
  • Unsettled balances automatically enter a 12-month custodial lock-up cycle.

CND Design & Reserve Framework

  • CND is designed as a USD-referenced, privacy-preserving casino exchange and settlement token.
  • CND may be converted into local currency, casino chips, or approved ecosystem services through participating venues and channels.
  • CND reserve coverage targets a 110% minimum aggregate risk-adjusted reserve value.
  • CNYT itself is not CND reserve collateral. Only contributed or contractually allocated assets may enter the reserve.

Staking, Distributions & Governance

  • Staking commitments: 6 months at 1.0x, 12 months at 1.5x, and 24 months at 2.5x distribution weight.
  • Eligible CNYT stakers may receive quarterly CND distributions from 20-40% of net CND operating fees.
  • Distributions begin after a six-month post-launch stabilization period.
  • Quarterly distribution proposals require 20% quorum of eligible active staked CNYT and simple majority approval.

Roadmap

  • October 2024: CNYT initial generation with 1B fixed supply.
  • December 15, 2024: 50% LONGRISE member ecosystem allocation.
  • 2026-2027: contract migration preparation, CND technology, reserve onboarding, privacy design, partner network, and security testing.
  • June 2027: target CNYT exchange listing, subject to readiness conditions.
  • Q1 2028: target CND launch.
  • Post-launch + 6 months: stabilization period, then quarterly fee distributions commence.
